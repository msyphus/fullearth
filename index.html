The Earth Is Full. Let's Learn How To Use It!

People always talk about earth being too full, as in too many people and not enough resources. We have a different viewpoint. The earth indeed is full, full of resources! The human race just needs to learn how to use them more responsibly and more creatively than in the past.



This site focuses on finding solutions to global food and water issues. There are simply too many people suffering from malnourishment, starvation, thirst, and contamination. The purpose of this site is to:

  • Provide educational material about important issues regarding food and water.
  • Provide instructional material about responsible home practices.
  • Conduct and report on research projects.
  • Host and promote meaningful service projects.
